About Our Academy

Since 2008, Nrutyashree Dance Academy has been preserving and promoting the ancient art of Bharatanatyam in Pune. Our academy focuses on maintaining the purity of this classical dance form while making it accessible to modern learners.

We provide a structured learning environment where students can immerse themselves in the rich traditions of Bharatanatyam, from basic steps to advanced choreography.
